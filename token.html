Introduction to NXC Token

The NXC token is the primary means of exchange within the Nexura ecosystem. NXC will be used to pay transaction fees, distribute returns generated from assets, and also be used to purchase future assets-backed coins. Initially, the NXC token will be available on the Binance Smart Chain, but once the new Nexura blockchain is launched, the NXC token will be transferred to the new blockchain.

Tokenomics

Total Supply

100,000,000 NXC tokens

Distribution

  • 10% (10,000,000 NXC) will be issued as tokens on the Binance Smart Chain:
    • 1% (1,000,000 NXC) for the team and advisors.
    • 1% (1,000,000 NXC) for marketers and advertisements.
    • 8% (8,000,000 NXC) will be sold to the community and investors in the ICO.
  • 90% (90,000,000 NXC) of the total supply will be mined after the official launch of the Nexura project and will not be issued as tokens.

After the ICO ends, the token will be available for free trading on PancakeSwap, allowing investors to fully benefit from the features of the token.

Opportunity for Early Investors

Each phase of the ICO gives investors a significant opportunity to achieve substantial returns, with the price increasing by 68% after each phase. Early investors who buy in the first phases will be able to acquire tokens at lower prices compared to those buying in the later phases.

End of ICO and Token Listing

After the completion of all ICO phases, the NXC token will be available for free trading on PancakeSwap. Additionally, the token will be transferred from the Binance Smart Chain to the new Nexura blockchain once the project is officially launched.
